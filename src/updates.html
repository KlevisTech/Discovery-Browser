New Features
📖
Read Mode New
A beautiful reader view that converts any web article into a clean, distraction-free reading experience. Supports custom fonts, dark mode, magic cursor for highlighting paragraphs, and saves articles for offline reading.
📝
Article Recap New
View all your saved articles and highlights in one dedicated window. Perfect for reviewing what you've read and revisiting important passages.
📄
Document Upload New
Upload and read documents (TXT, HTML, MD) in readmode style. Convert your local files into clean, readable articles.
📰
News Feed New
Stay informed with integrated news feeds. Click the news icon to browse and add cards for your favorite news sources.
⭐
Favorite Bookmarks New
Save your favorite sites for quick access. Right-click cards or use the star icon to mark favorites.
Customization
⚙️
Custom Site Layout Feature
In Settings, configure custom window layouts for specific websites. Set default size, position, and appearance for sites you visit often. Perfect for keeping your news or dashboard sites always visible the way you want.
🎨
Window Themes Feature
New theme options for your card windows. Choose from various color schemes to personalize your browsing experience.
Visualizer Improvements
🎵
Reactive Bubble Improved
The visualizer bubble now reacts when media is playing. Watch the colors dance and pulse in sync with your audio for an immersive experience.
🔒
Visual Lock Improved
Display the visualizer on your lock screen with beautiful animations. Keep the music visualization running even when your display sleeps.
